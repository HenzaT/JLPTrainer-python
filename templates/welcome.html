Welcome to the JLPTrainer backend

To find the kanji characters for each JLPT level, follow these links.

  • To see all JLPT kanji characters for a specific JLPT level (fill in the last number with a value between 1-5):

    • http://localhost:5000/api/index?level=jlpt-5

  • To see all JLPT kanji characters:

    • http://localhost:5000/api/all-kanji

  • To see details about a specific kanji (fill in the last character with your chosen kanji):
    • http://localhost:5000/api/show?kanji=七
